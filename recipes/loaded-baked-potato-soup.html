Loaded Baked Potato Soup

Home

The most delicious, hearty and comforting soup!

This creamy, flavorful soup takes a loaded baked potato to the next level.

It is the ultimate comfort meal that comes together in just one pot, making it a perfect dinner, any night of the week!

Ingredients

  • 6 strips of uncooked bacon, cut into small pieces
  • 3 tablespoons of unsalted or salted butter
  • 1 medium yellow onion, diced (about 1 cup)
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 1/b pounds of yukon gold potatoes, peeled and cut into 1 inch pieces
  • 4 cups of chicken broth
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 2/3 cups of heavy cream
  • 1/3 cup of all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon of red pepper flakes, if you like a little spice
  • Optional but incredible toppings:

    • Shredded cheddar cheese
    • Sliced green onions
